THE KINGDOM CONCEPT OF PREDESTINATION (Part 6)

Sub-Series Focus: How to Discover the Will of God

TEXTS: Romans 8:29-30

INTRODUCTION

Predestination is God’s predetermined plan for His children. To walk in His will, we must align with this plan and seek His guidance.

TODAY’S LESSON (5 Key Points)

  1. The Word of God as the Primary Guide
    God’s Word reveals His will for life and destiny. Study and meditation align your thoughts with His plan.
  2. The Witness of the Holy Spirit
    The Spirit gives inner confirmation and peace, guiding choices beyond logic.
  3. Prayer and Communion
    Prayer creates sensitivity to divine direction and keeps your heart aligned with God’s purpose.
  4. The Counsel of Godly Mentors
    Spirit-filled leaders provide wisdom that helps you discern God’s will.
  5. Circumstances and Divine Signals
    God can arrange events to confirm His plan, but test all against the Word and Spirit.

DAILY TAKEAWAY

Discovering God’s will requires Scripture, Spirit, prayer, wise counsel, and discernment.

SUMMARY

The will of God is revealed through His Word, confirmed by the Spirit, understood in prayer, supported by godly counsel, and affirmed by circumstances that align with Scripture.

PRAYER

Lord, open my eyes to Your will and grant me grace to walk in it daily. Amen.

DAILY BIBLE READING

Proverbs 3:5-6, Jeremiah 29:11, Ephesians 1:5.
