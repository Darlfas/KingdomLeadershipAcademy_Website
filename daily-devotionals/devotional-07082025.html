CHRIST IS THE REAL FOUNDATION (Part 4)

Sub-Series Focus: Knowing Christ As The Beginning And The End Is The Foundation

TEXTS: 2 Corinthians 1:20 (NLT), Colossians 2:9–10, John 11:25, Revelation 1:8, Hebrews 4:3, John 19:30, Ephesians 1:3–4, Romans 8:30

KEY SCRIPTURE

“For all of God’s promises have been fulfilled in Christ with a resounding ‘Yes!’ And through Christ, our ‘Amen’ (which means ‘Yes’) ascends to God for his glory.”

2 Corinthians 1:20 (NLT)

INTRODUCTION

Most believers are trapped in an endless waiting room—hoping for promises, breakthroughs, moves of God, or raptures to happen someday. But that mindset reveals one thing: they don’t truly know Christ as the Beginning and the End. The foundation of the Christian life is this: In Christ, all things are already finished. We’re not waiting for heaven to act—we’re waking up to what heaven already did. Jesus didn’t point to resurrection as an event—He declared “I AM the resurrection and the life” (John 11:25). In Him, everything has already happened. Faith is not hoping—it is aligning with what is already done.

TODAY’S LESSON (5-Point Summary)

  1. The Promises of God Are Not Pending
    Christ is not a pending transaction—He is the fulfillment of all that God ever intended.

    If it’s in Christ, it’s not in the future—it’s NOW.

  2. Jesus Is the End of Waiting
    He declared, “It is finished.” The old era of promises ended at the Cross.

    Stop standing on prophecy; start walking in fulfillment.

  3. You Are Not a Candidate—You Are Complete
    Colossians 2:10 says you are complete in Him. No missing piece. No next move. Just full access.

    Completion is your foundation—not struggle.

  4. Jesus Is the Rapture and the Life
    He didn’t promise Martha an event—He revealed a Person.

    Rapture is not a date on the calendar—it’s a dimension in Christ.

  5. The Foundation Is the Revelation of All-Things-Already
    You must grow into this consciousness daily: nothing is lacking, delayed, or denied in Christ.

    Religion waits. Revelation walks.

DAILY TAKEAWAY (SUMMARY)

The ignorance of Christ as the Foundation is why many are still pleading, sowing seeds, fasting endlessly, and waiting for what has already been given. This is not humility—it is blindness. Christ is not only the Beginning—He is also the End. In Him, your past is erased, your future is secured, and your present is loaded. You are not on a journey to Zion; you are already seated there (Hebrews 12:22). Everything God will ever do for you—He already did in Christ. It’s time to stop praying like beggars and start reigning like sons. Christ is not your Promise—He is your Portion.

PRAYER

Father, I renounce every lie that keeps me waiting for what is already finished in Christ. Open my eyes to see Jesus as the Beginning and the End of all things. I declare that I am not a seeker—I am a receiver. I am complete in Christ. The Cross ended the wait. The Resurrection began my reign. I now walk in the alreadiness of grace, power, and glory. In Jesus’ Name, Amen.

DAILY BIBLE READING

2 Corinthians 1:20, Colossians 2:9–10, John 11:25, Revelation 1:8, Hebrews 4:3, John 19:30, Ephesians 1:3–4, Romans 8:30.
